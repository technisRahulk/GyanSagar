About Gyansagar

Gyansagar is a social service wing of NIT Silchar, volunteered by the students of institute which is encouraged and approved by Prof. (Dr) N.V.Deshpande, Director, NIT Silchar. Since last five years, Gyansagar has put its effort towards the development of the society. The development is in terms of general education, IT education, health awareness, job-opportunity awareness, exploring the inherent qualities of kids in villages, etc. Gyansagar was started in the year 2010 by a group of students led by Mr. Aditya Choudhary, an electrical engineering 2013 passout student from NITS. Since then, it has taken huge leaps to become a social organization which benefits the nearby villages and communities near NIT Silchar. It had been headed by the dynamic leadership of Prof. (Dr) A. K. Sil, Professor, Department of Chemistry, NIT Silchar, since its establishment. We have around 300+ registered student volunteers every year. Undoubtedly, the whole hearted support of the student community of NIT Silchar is highly appreciated.

Message From Director


Since last ten years, Gyansagar has put its effort towards the development of the society. The development is in terms of general education, IT education, health awareness, job-opportunity, awareness, exploring the inherent qualities of kids in villages. Being Director Of National Institute of Technology Silchar, since December, 2017. I have observed Gyansagar which works as a family and puts tireless efforts for the betterment of the society. Contribution towards Gyansagar initiative by each individual member is the sole reason that it is growing even bigger and better with unfathomable determination to serve the deprived and under-privileged ones.

I Congratulate each and every student volunteers, teachers and people associated with it and wish to see Gyansagar reach greater heights.
Prof. Sivaji Bandyopadhyay
Director
National Institute of Technology Silchar Cachar, Assam.
